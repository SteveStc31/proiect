ABOUT

My name is Stefan Ciobotaru and I am a multi-disciplinary visual artist based in Cluj-Napoca.

Art has been part of my life since the beginning of my time, as I have always been intrigued by the aesthetics of my surroundings. my first contact with digital art was in 2017 and since that I have been constantly adapting my techniques and styles, in order to be more efficient in my work.
